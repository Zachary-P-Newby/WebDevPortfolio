Camera Selector

A sol is a day on Mars, the number in the sol selector represents nth sol on Mars after the arrival of the Curiosity Rover. Cameras will not always have photos taken on a given sol. Certain cameras, like the Mast, will have a large number of images that requires a significant loading time.
